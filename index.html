About Me

Who Am I?

Hello, I'm Loi and I am a recent graduate from UPNG where I obtained degree in Mathematics and Computer Science in 2022. In addition to my academic pursuits, I have a strong passion for leadership and volunteer work. Since 2018, I have been actively involved in the Peer Mentoring Program at UPNG, where I have made significant contributions to the program's success.

In 2019, I had the privilege of participating in the Career Trackers Program, an Australian Indigenous Internship Program, where I interned with Paradise Foods as an IT/Finance intern. During my time with Paradise Foods, I spent all my academic breaks working with the company, gaining valuable industry exposure and experience.


After completing my studies, I joined Paradise Company as an Assistant Production Analyst. In my current role, I am responsible for analyzing production data, planning, and scheduling productions. Working at Paradise Company has allowed me to apply my academic knowledge and practical skills in a real-world setting, and I am thrilled to be part of such a dynamic and forward-thinking organization.
